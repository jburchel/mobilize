Google OAuth Redirect URI Update Instructions

To fix the "Access blocked: Authorization Error" when signing in with Google, you need to update the authorized redirect URIs in the Google Cloud Console.

Step 1: Access the Google Cloud Console

Go to Google Cloud Console Credentials Page

Step 2: Find Your OAuth Client

Click on the OAuth 2.0 Client ID named "Mobilize CRM" or similar in the list of credentials.

Step 3: Add the Correct Redirect URI

In the "Authorized redirect URIs" section, add the following URI:

https://mobilize-crm.org/api/auth/google/callback
https://mobilize-crm-1069318103780.us-central1.run.app/api/auth/google/callback

Make sure to click the "ADD URI" button after pasting the URI.

Step 4: Save Your Changes

Click the "SAVE" button at the bottom of the page to save your changes.

Step 5: Test the Sign-In Flow

Go back to your application and try signing in with Google again.

The URL should be: https://mobilize-crm.org/api/auth/login-page

Note: It may take a few minutes for the changes to propagate through Google's systems.
